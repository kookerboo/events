Workshops

What is New & What is Coming

IT Transition to AtlasNet

Capacity Planning, Estimating & More

Moderator: Shirley Sveda, Senior Vice President, Atlas Canada

Facilitators: Ryan Parmenter, Director of IT, Atlas World Group & Tom Marquis, Director of IT, Atlas Canada

This session will be popular because Atlas will present two new platforms. First is new shipment tracking software piloted in the U.S. Once implemented in Canada next year, all network partners will be able to track the real-time status of Atlas-to-load shipments. Second, we will outline the new AtlasNet Estimating Platform that will go live in 2020 and enable you to create estimates on a contemporary interface. Don't miss this introduction to these important advancements!

Seasonal Demands? Volatile Labour Markets?

Proven Capacity Planning Tactics for Your Business

Moderator: Fred Haladay, Senior Vice President & Chief Commercial Officer, Atlas Canada

Facilitators: Enliven Consultants

Atlas agents have the difficult task of planning in a seasonal industry. Tight labour markets and operational coordination with OAs, DAs and the van line make the puzzle even more complex. Atlas Canada can help. This workshop will be lead by Jodi Zigelstein-Yip and Amy Altwerger-David from Enliven Consultants. Jodi and Amy will provide an interactive environment to outline the latest in capacity planning best practices. They will help you analyze your business to formulate the ideal human resource and equipment plan as you prepare for 2020.

Wellness in the Workplace

How Can You Reduce Absenteeism and Benefit Costs?

*Rollout of Owner Operators Benefit Program*

Facilitators: Bruce Tomkins & David Ogilvy of Ogilvy Insurance

In this session you will be introduced to the Wellness in the Workplace Program and the Owner Operator Benefit Program. Bruce, David and Lucky will take you through these robust programs and answer your questions so you can implement them in your business.

Sales & Marketing 2020

The latest in Lead Generation, Sales Tactics, and What's Next

Facilitators: Scott Clark, Manager, Marketing & Business Development, Atlas Canada and Jackie McCulloch, Intermediate Marketing Associate, Atlas Canada

Remember when you could buy a phone book ad and wait for the phone to ring? Yeah, it's much more complicated these days. In our industry, we can impact sales at two points on the sales cycle: 1) Generating the right volume of quality leads 2) Gaining customer trust to close the sale.

This session will give you actionable tactics to excel at these best practices. We will touch on Google rankings, social media and simple sales tactics that work. We will also launch new sales support pieces developed with the help of salespeople in the Atlas network who are in front of customers every day. Please join us at this workshop where we will simplify these concepts and give you new ideas to take back to your team.
